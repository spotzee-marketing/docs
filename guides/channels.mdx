Spotzee sends across six channels. Each channel uses a configured **provider** to do the actual delivery.

## Email

Transactional and marketing email. On Starter, bring your own delivery provider. On Professional and Enterprise, managed delivery infrastructure is included alongside any BYO provider you configure.

## SMS

Text messaging via your configured SMS provider. Per-message pricing applies on top of platform pricing.

## WhatsApp

WhatsApp Business messaging. Templates must be approved by WhatsApp before use.

## Push

Mobile push notifications via the standard mobile platforms.

## In-app

Messages rendered inside your own product, served via the API.

## Webhooks

Outbound HTTP callbacks. Use webhooks to forward events to your own systems, trigger third-party integrations, or update CRMs.

## Provider configuration

Providers are configured per project. A project can hold multiple providers per channel — Spotzee routes a send to the configured default unless you specify otherwise.
