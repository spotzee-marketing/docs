Blacklist Monitoring is a Tool that watches your sending infrastructure for blacklist appearances and alerts you the moment a listing happens. Add IPs and domains, pick how often they're checked, and Spotzee runs scheduled checks against 70+ real-time blacklists (RBLs) plus domain-based blacklists (DBLs) on the domain side.

<Info>
Blacklist Monitoring lives under **Tools** in the project sidebar and uses credits from your organisation's balance. The tool is read-and-write: you add resources, run checks, and configure groups; Spotzee handles the actual checking and alerting.
</Info>

## Why monitor blacklists

Email deliverability is reputation-sensitive. A single listing on a major RBL can drop a sender's inbox placement to zero overnight, often without any obvious symptom from the sender's side until campaigns start failing.

The integration:

- **Watches IPs** (IPv4) against major RBLs.
- **Watches domains** against domain-based blacklists.
- **Schedules checks** on each resource by group (daily or weekly).
- **Sends digest alerts** within an hour of a new listing being detected.
- **Tracks history** over a rolling 30-day window so you can see whether a listing was a one-off or an ongoing issue.

## Open the Tool

<Steps>
<Step title="Open Tools">
Inside the project, open **Tools** from the sidebar.
</Step>

<Step title="Pick Blacklist Monitoring">
Choose **Blacklist Monitoring**. You'll land on the **Overview** tab if monitoring is already enabled, or a setup screen if it isn't.
</Step>
</Steps>

## The Overview tab

The Overview is your at-a-glance health view. Four statistics sit at the top:

| Stat | What it carries |
|---|---|
| **Total IPs** | The number of IPs you're monitoring |
| **Total domains** | The number of domains you're monitoring |
| **Currently listed** | How many resources are blacklisted right now |
| **Checks today** | Total checks run in the past 24 hours |

A 30-day history chart sits below the stats showing listing counts over time. Sustained spikes mean a recurring listing problem; isolated bumps usually mean a transient listing that cleared.

## The four tabs

The Tool has four tabs:

| Tab | What's there |
|---|---|
| **Overview** | Stats cards plus the 30-day history chart |
| **IPs** | The list of monitored IPs, their statuses, and per-IP actions |
| **Domains** | The list of monitored domains, their statuses, and per-domain actions |
| **Settings** | RBL type selection, default notifications, group management |

## Credit usage

Blacklist checks consume platform credits from your organisation's balance. Each check costs roughly the same regardless of resource type. Keep an eye on your credit balance under **Billing** to avoid alerts pausing because credits ran out.

For more on credits, see [Billing and credits](/guides/billing-and-credits).
