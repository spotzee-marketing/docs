This guide walks you through sending your first campaign on the **Starter** tier. You'll create an account, import a small list, build a one-step journey, and send.

<Info>
  Starter is free up to 250 users and uses your own delivery providers (BYO). To use Spotzee's managed delivery infrastructure or financial-services features, contact sales about Professional or Enterprise.
</Info>

## Prerequisites

- A Spotzee account. [Sign up at spotzee.com/sign-up](https://spotzee.com/sign-up).
- A delivery provider account (any supported email or SMS vendor).
- A test user list (CSV or copy-pasted addresses).

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your organisation">
    After signing in, you'll be prompted to create an **organisation**. This is the top-level container that holds your projects, users, and billing.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Connect a delivery provider">
    From your project, open **Settings → Integrations** and add your email or SMS sending account. You'll be asked for the credentials your provider issues, usually an API key.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Import users">
    Open the **Users** section and upload a CSV. The first row should be column headers. Spotzee maps standard fields (email, phone, first name) automatically and stores the rest as custom attributes.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create a campaign">
    Open **Campaigns** and create a new campaign. Pick a channel, choose or compose a template, and select your imported users as the audience.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Send and monitor">
    Send the campaign. Delivery, opens, and clicks update on the campaign's detail page.
  </Step>
</Steps>
